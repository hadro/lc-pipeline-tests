We offer special advantages to
churches, benevolent and charitable as-
sociations, secret societies and holders of
trust funds. On all deposits received from
these sources we pay interest at from 4 to
6 per cent. per annum, regulated by the
length of time the deposits are allowed to
remain uncalled for.
Banks are made strong in proportion as
they are supported by the people.

Interest on Time Deposits will be as Follows:

On all sums of not less than $5—on deposit for
six months, next preceding the first day of
January or the first day of July, 2 1/2 per
cent per annum.

On all sums of not less than $50—on deposit for
six months, next preceding the first day of
January or the first day of July, 4 per cent
per annum.

On all sums of not less than $100—on deposit
for not less than one year, next preceding
the first day of January or the first day of
July, 6 per cent per annum.

Interest will be computed and placed to
the credit of depositors by the twentieth day of
January and July of each year.
