THE CAPITAL SAVINGS BANK

Presents the "compliments of the season" to its many patrons and friends, and asks for the New Year a continuance of the patronage which they have so generously given to it in the past, and upon which has been built the splendid success which the Bank has achieved.

With the purchase of our new building at 609 F St., at a cost of $32,000, and with the alterations necessary to adapt it to the needs of our business, we have greatly improved our facilities for transacting a general banking business, and we cordially invite the public to give us a larger share of their patronage and support.
