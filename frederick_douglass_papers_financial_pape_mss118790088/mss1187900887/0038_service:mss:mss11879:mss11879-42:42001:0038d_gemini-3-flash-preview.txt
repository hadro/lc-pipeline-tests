OFFICE HOURS.
From 9 o'clock A. M. to 3 P. M. on all days not legal holidays,
excepting on the First, Fifteenth and Last day of each month,
when the office will be kept open, for the receipt of deposits only,
until 4.30 P. M., but when those dates fall upon legal holidays the
office hours of the next business day will be extended until that time.
Saturdays being legal half holidays, the office of this Company
will, under the law, close at 12 o'clock noon.
