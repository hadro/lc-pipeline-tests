REV. WILLIAN M. PATTON, Founder and
President of the Patton Novelty Company;
Founder, Manager and Editor of the Bulletin
Publishing Company; Founder and Right Wor-
shipful Supreme Grand Master of the Improv-
ed Order of Craftsmen and Princesses of
Honor. He is also a line member and Supreme
Representative of the Universal Brotherhood,
the Knights and Daughters of Labor, the
Knights and Ladies of Honor, the Knights of
Honor of the World. He is also inventor of
several valuable preparations which are being
widely used. The Rev. is an ordian elder of

sas and Missouri Conference, and Station
