To the Members of the
Masonic Craft of this Jurisdiction:
We have long felt that a necessity of our organization was
a good Masonic Directory, giving the names of the Officers
of our Grand and Subordinate bodies, including those of
the Ladies of the Order of the Eastern Star and Courts of
Heroines of Jericho, with the names and post office addresses
of the entire Craft.
We have with considerable labor tried to respond to this
necessity, and have compiled this little directory for presen-
tation to the members of the Craft and Adoptive rites of Ma-
sonry in this Jurisdiction.
It contains the names and addresses of the members of
our Thirteen Blue Lodges, Five Royal Arch Chapters, Four
Knights Templar Commanderies, A. A. S. R. Consistory
Mecca Temple, A. A. O. N. Mystic Shrine, Six Chapters of
the Order of the Eastern Star, and Two Courts of the He
roines of Jericho.
We have labored hard to make this book as nearly correct
as possible, and we desire each brother and sister to ha
a copy, with the hope that it may be the means of bringin
into closer touch every branch of our beloved Order, an
forming a more perfect union, so that we may all be in fac
united in one sacred band or society of friends, brothers and
sisters, among whom no contention shall ever arise.
I present this directory to the Craft with my compliments,
and with the hope that the object desired may be attained.
Fraternally submitted:
MALVIN,
Grand Master.
