TELEPHONE BUSINESS OFFICE
The Telephone Business Office for the Caraway and Monette exchanges is located in the Monette,
Arkansas telephone central office building. The hours of operation for this office are from 8:00 a.m. un-
til 12:00 noon, and 1:00 p.m. until 5:00 p.m., daily, Monday through Friday. Payments may be made at
the Business Office, by mail, or by using the convenient Caraway or Monette depositories.
You are always welcome to stop in and discuss your telephone business matters or to inquire about
the many additional telephone facilities which can make your service more useful and convenient.
