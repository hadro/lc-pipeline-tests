62

DESSERTS.

ORANGE PUDDING.

One lb. of sponge cake, sliced thin; pour over it
one pint of cream or milk; let it stand until soft;
grate into it the rind of two oranges and the juice of
one; four eggs well beaten; half a cup of sugar.
Bake it until a light brown.

CHOCOLATE JELLY CAKE, No. 1.

Two cups of sugar; half a cup of butter; one cup
of milk; three eggs; two cups of flour; one tea
spoonful of cream of tartar, and half a teaspoonful
of soda.

FOR JELLY.

One cup of sugar; one table-spoonful of corn starch;
two table-spoonfuls of grated chocolate; let it boil
until it is thick; then flavor it with vanilla. Put
it between the cakes while warm.
