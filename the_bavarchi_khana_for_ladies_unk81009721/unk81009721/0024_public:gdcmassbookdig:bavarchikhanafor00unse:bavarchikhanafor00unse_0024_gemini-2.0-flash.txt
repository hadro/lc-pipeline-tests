CHOW CHOW.
One peck of green tomatoes. Six peppers, taking out
the seeds. Three onions. Chop together and cover them
over night, adding two-thirds of a cup of salt. In the
morning, draw off the liquor and just cover with vinegar.
add one and a half cups of sugar. Less than a cup of
made mustard. A table-spoon of cloves, allspice and cin-
namon. Boil all about twenty minutes or more.
Much improved by the addition of cauliflower and cel-
ery, with the other things.
