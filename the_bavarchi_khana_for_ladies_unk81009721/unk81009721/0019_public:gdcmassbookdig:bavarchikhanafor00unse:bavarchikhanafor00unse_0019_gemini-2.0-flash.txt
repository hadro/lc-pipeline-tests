DELICATE CAKE.
Whites of three eggs. One and a half cups of sugar.
Half a cup of butter. Three cups of flour. One cup of
milk. Half a tea-spoon of soda. One tea-spoon of cream
tartar. One tea-spoon of extract of almond. Use the
yolks in the same way.

SPONGE CAKE.
One lb. of eggs. One lb. of sugar. Half a lb. of flour.
Beat the eggs separately.
