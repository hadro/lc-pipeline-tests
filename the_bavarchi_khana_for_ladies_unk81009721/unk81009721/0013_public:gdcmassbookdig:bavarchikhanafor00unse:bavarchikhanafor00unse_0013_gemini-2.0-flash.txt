Desserts.

ORANGE PUDDING.

To two large oranges peeled and picked to pieces, add
a cup of sugar. Boil a quart of milk mixed with the yolks
of three eggs, and two dessert-spoons of corn-starch.
Cool it and stir into it the orange and sugar. Cover it
with meringue made of the whites of the eggs, and a little
sugar, and brown in the oven.
