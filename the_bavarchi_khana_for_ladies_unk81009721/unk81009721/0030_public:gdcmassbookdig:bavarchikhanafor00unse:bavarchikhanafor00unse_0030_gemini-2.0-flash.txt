DESSERTS.

ORANGE PUDDING.

Boil one dozen of oranges until they are soft;

take out the seeds, and chop the rind very fine;

put to it six eggs well beaten, one-quarter of a

pound of melted butter, one-quarter of a pound of

sugar; bake it one hour.

CHOCOLATE JELLY CAKE, No. 1.

One-half cup of butter; one and one-half cup

of sugar; one-half cup of sweet milk; two and one-

half cups of flour; two eggs; one teaspoonful of

cream of tartar; one-half teaspoonful of soda.

FOR JELLY.

Take one-half cake of chocolate, grated, and

one-half cup of milk; let it come to a boil; add

one-half cup of sugar, one-half teaspoonful of

vanilla, and spread on the cake.

JELLY FOR THE ABOVE.

One cup of sugar; one-half cup of butter; one-

fourth cup of milk, and one-half cup of flour.
